The photographer:


Shaul Y. Khayyat is a student at the prestigous Lander College for Men, where he is currently studying Talmud and Computer Science. Nearly ten years ago, Shaul discovered a passion and talent for photography. In the years since, he has travelled to some of the most scenic locations in New York, New Jersey, Toronto, and Jerusalem. As a yeshiva bochur, Shaul has a unique perspective and view which comes through in his photos.




The equipment:


All pictures were taken with a Panasonic Lumix FZ200. Editing was done both in-camera, as well as on Photoscape X and Windows Photo Editor.
